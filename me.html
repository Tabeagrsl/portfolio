Tabea Grösel

I’m 23 and grew up in a beautiful small town in Germany. At the age of 16 me and my family moved to Norway. Moving abroad at such a young age made me less afraid to leave home behind and explore new places and cultures. After my school time I worked for a travel agency in Greece and Spain. During the last two winters I escaped to the South-East-Asia and backpacked my way through Thailand, Laos, Cambodia, Vietnam, China, the Pillippines and Singapore.
Last autumn I started studying Multimedia Design at KEA in Copenhagen. In my first Semester I got introduced to a lot of new subject areas and technologies. I'm happy to say that after my first 5 months I am still eager to learn about the complexity of Media and Technology.
My favorite color is blue, I have a serious addiction to cupcakes and snorkeling is my number one hobby.
