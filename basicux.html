Theme 04. Basic UX

During our 4th Theme: Basic UX we learned about Design Sprint. I worked in a team of 6 with the goal to create and test a realistic prototype within one week. Our long-term goal was to make the management learning system fronter a one in all solution for students and teachers. The thinking out loud test was used to collect data from students that used fronter daily. We choose this test over a trunk test or 5-second test because we could collect more detailed user experiences. Later I used the google invented Sprint method again in relation to create a Native App.
